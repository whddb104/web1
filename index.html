K-pop singer Kim Sang-woo, better known as Roy Kim, begins his mandatory military service as a marine, today.

He will start boot camp training for the Marine Corps at a training center in Pohang, North Gyeongsang. After seven weeks of training, he will be stationed. Kim's agency Stone Music Entertainment announced in May at the singer had applied to enlist in the Marines in his own will.
